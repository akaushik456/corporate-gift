About The Team

With an objective to transform people’s perception of gifts and revolutionize the gifting culture, bigsmall.in was founded by two brothers, Aman and Yatin Hans and took its first steps in May 2015. Since then, Ferns n Petals.in has dedicated itself to deliver high-quality unique gifts to its customers by making gifting a blithesome experience for them. We realized gifting has turned into a lost art. Thus, with a goal to revive this culture of celebration and turning every day into an occasion, Ferns n Petals.in brought in like-minded people together.Today, we are a 15 member team comprising of Ops head, marketing team, content writers, Accounts team, interns and packaging boys. For our shipping & logistics requirements, we tied up with shipping aggregators and use only reputed delivery partners such as FedEx, BlueDart and Delhivery as we strive to give you the best gifting experience.Starting with mere 50 products, the store now hosts 600+ unique and unusual gifts on the website. Every gift on our shelf is one-of-a-kind, to match the unique and quirky personality of the recipient. From placing the order to its packaging, our team ensures complete customer satisfaction, taking care of little things that are often missed out in the rush for perfection. This apparently is the reason for our super content customers, who love not just the products but also our service.
